Massivearts Music Studio Milan

In the end of 2016 I've been commisioned to develop a CMS Wordpress Site for the most important Music Studio in Milan, called Massivearts. I worked in theme with a photographer, to get the best pictures result of that music environment. I customized a Wordpress theme with custom CSS and the use of plugins (I used Wordpress Social Stream for the social news, that connects the Website to the FB official page; and I used WPML to implement the multilanguage site).
