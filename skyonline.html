Sky Online Italy

In 2015 I worked in collaboration with Razorfish Agency in Milan, for the development of their new DEM campaign for the Sky Online new website
(the company changed the name last year in Now TV).
I had to develop the graphic for the slider background in the homepage, and the graphic for the newsletter and email marketing. First I worked on the mockup in Photoshop, and once the wireframe was accepted I developed the code in HTML and CSS.
(the email were sent to 200k people a day).
