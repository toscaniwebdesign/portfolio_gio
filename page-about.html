Something about me


I'm 31, from Italy. I graduated in Product Design in 2013 (5 years course at Politecnico di Milano), and immediately after having graduated I began to devote myself to the study of the Wordpress CMS.

In the university of Product Design I've learned the Design Process Strategy, from the first idea to the final production of a specific product for a specific user target; I always bring these skills in my web side, expecially for the User Experience and the User Interface.

In late 2013 I started an internship as a Graphic Designer and Frontend Designer in Mediatria studio, where I learned HTML and CSS, and some JavaScript language(jQuery especially).

After this collaboration, which lasted a year, in 2014 I started working as a freelancer in Milan, where for three years I have been developing websites for small and medium clients.
At the end of 2015 I worked in collaboration with the International Communication Agency Razorfish in Milan for 6 months, to follow the SKY Online newsletter and email marketing Campaign for their new online streaming website (they recently changed the name in Now TV).

I speak fluent English and Portoguese(I've spent one year in Rio de Janeiro in 2011, working as Product Designer at Lattoog Design Studio for 6 months), and a bit of Spanish. I play guitar and I love composing music in my free time with Logic Pro X.
